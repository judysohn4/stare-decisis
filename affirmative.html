Affirmative Action

Affirmative action or not? Catching up with what teens should know about the long-standing policy in college admissions.

By: Jaimie Chun
December 31st, 2022

What is affirmative action?
   Affirmative action is a policy that focuses on increasing workplace or educational opportunities for underrepresented individuals. Affirmative action programs involve taking into account ethnicity, socioeconomic background, sex and national origin in admissions or hiring process.
  •    Purpose
   Affirmative action attempts to diversify various aspects of society and focuses on demographics who have historically been less represented in leadership, professional and academic roles.
